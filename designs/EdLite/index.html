" Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of mind to think "

We are EdLite Inspirations

A ONE2ONE Learning Institute for ALL Levels and Subjects. An environment that appreciates every student’s questioning ability by training of the mind to think than learning of facts.

Is Learning fun? The single unanswerable question posed by kids today!

Burdened by studies, many students see learning as a great hurdle. Math nicknamed MMs (MONSTER MaTH).

To inspire the students for core fundamental learning, we give individual attention through our ONE2ONE learning environment. Our curriculum is designed to adapt student needs in a way everyone progresses.

Our EdLite teachers, are well experienced MOE Registered teachers with interactive blend in skills and will make sure every student has a comfortable learning experience.

Live feedback system with SAME DAY reporting to Parents on student’s performance and tracking improvises the chance of our kid’s success.
