ACT

For students who wish to take on the ACT examination, we offer help as well, certainly from teachers with ample experiences and knowledge. We will guide the students to be able to understand the English section and score well in the reading comprehension section by understanding the passage through exposure to different types of possible passages.

Not only English, we will also train our students to work under the pressure and time chase for Mathematics, meticulously making sure the students are able to withstand the possible pressure while thinking quickly in order to make sure they have enough time to answer all of the questions. We will also help the student to learn and study the Science section based on the given curriculum, and train them to be able to think on their feet and score as many points as possible within the limited time duration.

SAT

We offer help from our experienced teachers for students who wish to pursue their studies abroad and need to take the SAT examinations.

We train our students to be able to score in the English section — be it reading or writing — by familiarizing them to the standardized reading comprehension passages as well as how to answer the required writing section and how to craft a brilliant essay, we will also condition the students to be able to do mental calculation on a faster speed so as to get used to doing the Mathematics section which strictly requires no calculator usage, while also training them to be able to apply the formulas and think critically in order to solve the Mathematics section as a whole.

A ONE2ONE Learning Institute for ALL Levels and Subjects. An environment that appreciates every student’s questioning ability by training of the mind to think than learning of facts.
